Strip-Silk-Made Ball

As a significant element of traditional Chinese culture, the strip-silk-made ball embodies rich folkloric and historical significance. It is a testament to the craftsmanship and creativity of artisans, with each piece reflecting their dedication and skill. More than an art form, the ball serves as a cultural symbol, carrying aspirations for harmony, prosperity, and joy. Traditionally exchanged during weddings and festivals, it conveys blessings and communal values.
